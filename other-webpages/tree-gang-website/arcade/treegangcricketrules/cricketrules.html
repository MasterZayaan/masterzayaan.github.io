The Tree Gang Cricket rules

First, before you start Cricket, read some info down there.

The Tree Gang Cricket game info

System requirements:

Devices with touch screens such as the iPad and iPhone and Android devices should be compatible with this game.

You can also use desktops and PCs as well.

How to play:

To play, press the cricket button and when the ball comes near you, press the button again and keep doing this repeatedly.

The higher the ball goes, the more points you have. The game also saves your points next time you visit the website.

Use your mouse pointer on PCs and desktops, and use your good old fingers for mobile devices.

And finally have fun!

To start the game press the Start Cricket! button.
